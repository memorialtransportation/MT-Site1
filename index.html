About Memorial Transportation

Memorial Transportation LLC is an Atlanta-based carrier focused on time-sensitive freight, airport cargo linehaul, and dedicated lane operations. We serve freight forwarders, airlines, integrators, and structured freight networks with reliable communication, speed, and consistent execution.


Our Atlanta hub gives us strategic access to major freight corridors including ATL, ORD, DFW, IAD, California, the Northeast, Southeast, Southwest, and nationwide destinations.

For airport linehaul and expedited freight where cut times matter, team drivers provide the speed and consistency needed to meet tight delivery windows.
